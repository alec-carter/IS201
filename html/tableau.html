1) How are profits faring in the 50 states of the United States?

As you can see from the Tableau visualization above profits are faring the best In California and New york. They are actually losing money in places such as Texas and Colorado. The darker blue the state is the more profit they are making. The orange states are losing money.

2) How has one of the highest-profit states fared for the last two years by profit, shown by month?

The state I chose that had high profits was California. The data above displays the monthly profit for california from January of 2013 to December of 2014. It appears that profits increase towards the end of each year.

3) How are profits within that state doing for each product category?

The following graph shows the profit each sub category in california has made. Accessories appear to have made the most profit.

4) For the most profitable category, forecast sales (pick a state that has enough data for a forecast) through November 2015

The Most profitable category in California was Accessories. It is forecasted that for the next 11 months they will make an average of $1,211 a month. This will obviously vary month by month.
